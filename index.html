Signify


Swiss-Knife for hearing and/or speech impaired people

A mobile application that aims to solve social problems regarding communication, understanding and expressing for the hearing impaired and/or speech impaired people

Show More



Clean and simple, Comes with everything you need!

Realtime Translate

The main purpose of Signify is to provide a channel among hearing-impaired or speech-impaired people and society. The application translates a speech or text in English to Sign language through the instrumentality of an animation for continuous visualization.

One Device, Everywhere

With Signify, users can easily communicate through video calls, as well as use their mobile phones in order to real time translate to both text and Sign language in a day-to-day conversation.

Cautious Every Time

Signify also help impaired people to identify urgent issues happening around them. Signify will vibrates the phone in different types for hearing/speech impaired people to become aware of the major voices such as sirens and car horns.
